WORKSHOPS - SAFETY TALK AND FLIGHTS


The Safety Talk and Flights are Flight Safety workshops organized by the Aero Clube de Mirandela, where relevant topics are discussed, approaching theoretical concepts and their practical application.

-Briefing with weather and NOTAMs of the day;
-Emergency of the Day;
-Accident/Incident report analysis;
-Theoretical presentation;
-Journal Club;
-Flights with instructor.

SURVEILLANCE FLIGHTS


To minimize impact to other airspace users, only a selected number of subareas L, N and S will be activated daily for a period of 6 to 9 hours. For information updated on active subareas contact Lisbon Information on frequencies 130.905MHZ, 123.755MHZ or 131.055MHZ or by calling (+351) 218 ​​553 462.
Areas that directly affect LMPI: N and N, which are exactly vertical from Mirandela.

-N9 starts above 2000'AGL, i.e. 3500'MSL
-N starts from the ground up to 7500'. Fly within this area after contacting and coordinating the flight with the deployment of the FAP via radio.

OPERATIONS


UAS operations in LPMI:
1 - Take off from runway 17/35
2 - After take-off, they return to the East (E) and remain in orbit rising until 2000'
3 - They move to the West (W) side of the runway and continue in orbit climbing to 5500'
4 - After reaching cruising altitude, they head to the work areas of greatest interest (see NOTAM).
The ascent takes between 5 to 10 minutes and during this period the track will be closed.
UAS make 1-2 flights a day, stay in flight for about 6 hours
The FAP will be monitoring 122.205 MHz, radio communications must always be maintained
The callsign of the UAS is: CYCLOP3
FAP contacts are available on the school classroom board for
coordinate some flight if necessary.
The operation is daily.
