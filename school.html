ULTRALIGHT SCHOOL


The Ultralight Piloting School was born on October 14, 2009 and since then already trained more than students. Since its creation it has been an asset to the Aero Clube de Mirandela, contributing to the great growth in the number of pilots who regularly fly our aircraft. Offers PILOT training OF ULTRALIGHT, conversion of PPA/PCA/PLAA licenses and into RADIOTELEPHONY.

At the moment Aero Clube de Mirandela has 40 pilots and students in its ranks and per year they fly about 350 flight hours. These are considerable numbers, of which many we are proud of and that we want to increase even more by opening new ULTRALIGHT PILOT courses.

THEORICAL INSTRUCTION


The Theoretical Instruction is carried out in the modest, but modern, facilities of the flying club where students can find all the conditions to attend classes, study and do their your flight plans.

The Theoretical Instructors are, for the most part, in-house pilots and instructors with great experience in ultralight, light and airline aviation. Our didactic material was entirely designed by our instructors and is of superior quality.

PRATICAL INSTRUCTION


Practical Instruction takes place at the Mirandela Municipal Airfield, whose conditions are excellent for the practice of this sport modality, at the controls of the modern Tecnam P-92 EAGLET, Tecnam P-92 Super and Fantasy Air Allegro 2000. Flight Instructors are pilots with several thousand hours in civil and military aviation, with a lot of experience in the field of flight instruction. We have excellent quality practical manuals (Checklist, Contact Manual, Navigation Manual, Standardization Manual, VFR Manual, etc.) where our students can discover the details of each maneuver so that they can perform it in complete safety.

QUALIFICATIONS


The School is licensed to provide the following training courses: - Ultralight Pilot Course in Group 3 Advanced Multi-axle Class (MEA-G3)
- Conversion of the airplane license (PPA, PCA, PLAA) into ULM
- Renewal / Revalidation of ULM license
- Flight Instructor Qualification
- Renewal of Instructor Qualification
- Conversion of Airplane Instructor license to ULM Instructor
- Qualification MEB-G2 to MEA-G3
- Foreign PU license conversion


At our Aero Clube, we attach great importance to detail and we are careful to have our aircraft always in optimal airworthiness conditions. We invite all those who have a special taste for flying, and want to get to know this sporting modality, to visit the Aero Clube de Mirandela for a guided tour of our facilities and carry out an outreach flight.

COURSES
