Flying

Flight Batism

“Once you have experienced flying, you will walk across the earth with eyes turned toward heaven, for you were there and there you will want to return."
Leonardo da Vinci

The publicity flight consists of a flight of approximately 15-20 minutes, in the company of an experienced pilot from the Aero Clube de Mirandela. Come with us to explore Mirandela from the air, the surrounding Trás-os-Montes landscapes, with unique glimpses of the rivers and dams. We suggest a donation of €30 per publicity flight.

We also do panoramic flights, with routes that generally include Mirandela, Tua river, Douro river, Régua, Mirandela. The cost of this varies depending on the flight time (150€/hour).

Let yourself be captivated by the passion for flying and offer an unforgettable experience to family and friends. friends. At the end, you will receive a flight baptism certificate.
