About Us

Aeroclube de Mirandela

INFRASTRUTURES

ACM have it's headquarters at the Aeródromo Municipal de Mirandela .
The installations are modest, but operational enough to guarantee SAFE operation to all practitioners of the various aeronautical modalities.
The "Aeródromo Municipal de Mirandela" includes an asphalted runway 750 meters long and with a hangar large enough to accommodate 4 ultralight aircraft (240 m2 15x16 m; Door 13 x 2.9m);

In addition to its library, Aeroclub also offers:


Classroom with various audiovisual support material, Operations Room equipped with up-to-date charts and manuals for flight planning, Meteorological station with Webcam, Bar and Internet access. The Aeroclub provides in its library several manuals, official publications, books and magazines, which can be consulted.


In this way, we intend to foster a taste for aeronautical culture and contribute to the continuous training of our associates.
